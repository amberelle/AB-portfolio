Workfrom Editorial Re-Design

Working as an intern for Workfrom, I was tasked with redesigning their editorial content.

In an effort to streamline and encourage browsing and searching on their editorial content, Workfrom wanted a new design layout that incorporated the different types of content while keeping a similar style. I met with staff regularly to get each new stage of wireframes and designs approved. At the end of the short internship I was able to give them my designs.
